About Me

My name is Aidan Allajbej, and I am a student who is passionate about technology, problem solving, and creative work. A large part of my academic focus is on data science, programming, and learning how computers can be used to solve real problems in efficient and meaningful ways. I enjoy understanding not only how code works, but also how systems, websites, and software are designed to interact with people. This is one reason I find web development interesting. Building a website combines structure, design, and communication into one project, and it gives me the opportunity to present information in a way that feels personal and engaging.

Outside of class, I spend a lot of time exploring technology and hardware. I like learning about computers, performance optimization, gaming peripherals, and how different software tools can improve productivity. I am also interested in academic growth and enjoy courses that challenge me to think critically and build useful projects. What I like most about this field is that there is always something new to learn. Whether I am working on a coding assignment, experimenting with data visualization, or trying to improve a technical skill, I like the process of slowly getting better through practice. This website is a simple way to introduce who I am and highlight a few of the interests that motivate me both inside and outside the classroom.
