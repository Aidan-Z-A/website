About Me

Hi, I am Aidan Allajbej, and I am a student currently studying data science at Northeastern. I am somewhat passionate about tech problem solving, and creative work. A large part of my academic focus is on data science but I spend a majority of my time in other fields such as CSec and even the Arts. However, programming is still a cornerstone to my path as a student. I have especially enjoyed learning how computers can be used to solve real problems in efficient and meaningful ways outside of the classroom! I enjoy understanding not only how code works, but also how systems, websites, and software are designed to interact with people.

Outside of class, I enjoy spending a good deal of time exploring technology and hardware. On the hardware side I like delving into, performance/optimization, gaming peripherals, and the tools that help developers build useful software. I also enjoy building projects like this website because it allows me to combine some of my creativity with the technical skills we have gathered thus far. If I had to say when my programming career started it would have to be from the first time I played a computer game on my old WindowsXP; you know the one with the rolling green hills :).
