My Interests

Technology and Learning

Tech has always been something that interests me because it combines problem solving with creativity. Whether I am programming or doing something else exploring new I have always enjoyed understanding how different systems work and blend together. This curiosity is what led me to code and what has been driven me to data science in my academic studies.

Outside of coursework, I typically spend a large majority of time drawing and playing music. While this may seem counterintuitive for a data scientist I do not really see a world in which I do not do these things. I have found that having these two outputs has increased my efficiency in both professional and personal projects. I find that in a field like technology where evolution is a constant having a way to step back and see the world with a clear view is incredibly important. In turn, these unrelated hobbies made it exciting to stay engaged and continue learning in school and university.

Prior to attending Uni. I had always thought of being a business student instead. Because of this I still share an interests in subjects related to quant/finance and this has helped me greatly in understanding what I want to get out of my experience taking these Data Science classes.

Some things I enjoy

  • Programming and software development
  • Computer hardware and performance tuning
  • Gaming and analyzing competitive play
  • Learning new technologies
  • Building small technical projects
